The samburu beads

Once the relatives of the girl (mother and brothers) agree with the moran on the relationship, the moran buys the girl beads and she has to wear them as you can see in the photo.Her wearing the beads is a sign that she is beaded and nobody else can engage in  a sexual relationship with her.
